What is a “bad hire”?

They may be a wonderful person, but a “bad hire” is someone who does not fit your company culture and/or the role.

  • Key Report Data must be completed before entering data for the calculator to work and provide a report.
  • Hire the Best uses a pro sports metaphor. Job candidates = “Players;” Employees = “team members;” Companies, Divisions, and Workgroups = “teams.”
  • Make certain to include all the costs, so you are realistically estimating your full hiring expenses.
